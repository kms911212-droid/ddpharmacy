Product Description
Reju de vie Repair Cream is a skin-repairing cream formulated with 1,300 ppm PDRN, collagen, and exosomes, designed to provide a triple-synergy formula that supports skin regeneration, soothing care, and hydration.
The formula focuses particularly on restoring skin affected by UV exposure, moisture loss, and weakened skin barrier, which are common concerns during the summer season.
Repeated exposure to strong sunlight may lead to reduced elasticity, dryness, and weakened skin barrier function. In such cases, simple moisturization alone may not be sufficient for optimal skin recovery.
By combining 1,300 ppm PDRN with collagen and exosomes, the cream helps support the skin’s regenerative environment while providing soothing and moisturizing care.
With regular use, it helps improve overall skin texture and promotes healthier, more resilient-looking skin.
Key Ingredients
PDRN (1,300 ppm)
Derived from salmon DNA, PDRN helps support skin regeneration and recovery while soothing irritated skin.
Low Molecular Collagen
Helps improve skin elasticity and maintain smoother, healthier-looking skin.
Exosome
Supports cellular communication within the skin, helping enhance regeneration and improve the delivery of active ingredients.
How to Use
Cleansing → Toner / Essence → Reju de vie Repair Cream → (Daytime) Sunscreen
Apply an appropriate amount to the entire face or sensitive areas and gently press with your palms to aid absorption.
For intensive care, the cream can be used when the skin barrier feels weakened, after skin procedures, or during seasons with strong UV exposure such as summer.
